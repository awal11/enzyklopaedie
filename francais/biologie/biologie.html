Systeme Respiratoire

The respiratory system is responsible for us breathing and inhaling vital oxygen. It also has the task of expelling CO2. After inhaling the oxygen it is inserted into the blood, which takes the oxygen and other nutrients to tissues. While the tissue takes the oxygen from the blood, it gives carbon dioxide in the blood, which is taken to the lungs, the main organs of the respiratory system, and after that it’s expelled from the body.

Systeme Circulatoire

Every animal needs the circulatory system for living since they need blood to transport vital substances to each cell. The heart pumps blood into the body and into the lungs, so it's one of the most important organs of the human body.
