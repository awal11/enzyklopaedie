The Boletus edulis

The Boletus edulis (also known as cep or penny bun) is a species of the genus Boletus. Its average height is 25 cm, the cap's width is mostly around 20 cm. Penny bun is known for its small white pores that can get yellow as it ages. Its cap surface is white when young and brown when mature.The peak mushroom season takes place from summer to autumn, folklore mentions that the best time to find cep is a full moon.
