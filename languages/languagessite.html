Germanic Languages

The Germanic languages are a branch of the Indo-European language family and include languages such as English, German, and Swedish.

Romance Languages

The Romance languages are a group of languages descended from Latin, including French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Romanian and Catalan.

Slavic Languages

The Slavic languages are a group of closely related languages spoken in Central and Eastern Europe, including Russian, Polish, and Ukrainian.
